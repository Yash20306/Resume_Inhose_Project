Title: Data Analyst
Description: We are seeking a highly motivated and detail-oriented Data Analyst to join our growing team in Gujarat. In this full-time role, you will be instrumental in transforming raw data into actionable insights that drive strategic business decisions. You will leverage your expertise in Python and SQL to extract, clean, and analyze complex datasets, and then visualize your findings through compelling dashboards in Tableau. If you possess strong analytical skills, a passion for data, and the ability to communicate complex information clearly, we encourage you to apply.
Responsibilities: Extract, clean, and transform large datasets from various sources using SQL and Python., Perform in-depth data analysis to identify trends, patterns, and anomalies, providing actionable insights to stakeholders., Design, develop, and maintain interactive and user-friendly dashboards and reports using Tableau., Collaborate with cross-functional teams to understand business requirements and translate them into data analysis projects., Ensure data accuracy and integrity through rigorous data validation and cleaning processes., Present findings and recommendations to non-technical audiences in a clear and concise manner., Monitor key performance indicators (KPIs) and provide regular updates on business performance., Contribute to the continuous improvement of data analysis processes and tools.
Requirements: Proven experience (1+ year) as a Data Analyst or in a similar analytical role., Proficiency in SQL for data extraction and manipulation., Strong programming skills in Python for data analysis (e.g., Pandas, NumPy)., Demonstrable experience in building interactive dashboards and reports using Tableau., Excellent data cleaning, validation, and transformation skills., Ability to translate complex data into clear, actionable insights and recommendations., Strong communication and presentation skills, with the ability to explain technical concepts to non-technical stakeholders., Problem-solving mindset with a keen eye for detail., Ability to work independently and as part of a team in a fast-paced environment.
Skills: SQL (Advanced), Python (Pandas, NumPy), Tableau (Dashboard Development, Data Visualization), Data Cleaning and Preprocessing, Statistical Analysis, Data Storytelling, Business Intelligence, Microsoft Excel, Communication Skills, Analytical Thinking
Qualifications: Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, Economics, Business Analytics, or a related quantitative field., Minimum of 1 year of professional experience in data analysis., Portfolio or examples of previous data analysis projects or Tableau dashboards (preferred).
Location: Gujarat
Employment Type: Full-time
Experience: 1 year
